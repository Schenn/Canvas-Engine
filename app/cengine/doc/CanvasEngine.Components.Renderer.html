Class: Renderer

CanvasEngine.Components.Renderer

new Renderer(params, entity)

The Renderer component is responsible for managing the properties required to draw an object to a canvas. You can provide your own clear method if your shape is too irregular to clear with a rect If no clearInfo method is provided, the Renderer uses its x, y, height and width in a clearRect method.
